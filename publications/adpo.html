AdPO: Enhancing the Adversarial Robustness of Large Vision-Language Models with Preference Optimization

Progressive Journal · 2025

This paper introduces AdPO, a preference-optimized framework designed to enhance the adversarial robustness of large vision-language models (VLMs). By incorporating preference alignment mechanisms into adversarial fine-tuning, AdPO improves both interpretability and resilience across multimodal reasoning tasks.
