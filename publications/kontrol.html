Kontrol Lalu Lintas Jaringan Wi-Fi menggunakan Evil Limiter pada Kali Linux

Nabil Arif A’isy, David Zico Rafael Sitorus, Mohammad Hafiz Fachrezi Lubis, and Shelvie Nidya Neyman

Progressive Journal · 2024

This paper evaluates the use of Evil Limiter on Kali Linux as a tool for controlling and limiting Wi-Fi network traffic. The study discusses how bandwidth usage can be managed in local networks and examines the effectiveness of Evil Limiter in reducing excessive bandwidth consumption and improving network stability.

The paper also considers potential security risks associated with the use of ARP spoofing-based traffic control tools and emphasizes that such methods should be applied carefully in appropriate network-management contexts.
